Where did my username come from?

Wondering what "do-winter-for-6" or "dow-inter-for-6" means? Back in the early/mid 2000s, a popular game occupied most of my friends' free time - Runescape. A good friend of mine tried to get me into the game, but I struggled to come up with a creative username (much like everyone else), so I asked them for a suggestion, but I didn't quite make out what they said over the phone and just typed out what I thought I heard... It's now a very unique username that's never taken, and those that know me by that name just refer to me as "winter" as that's much easier and memorable.
